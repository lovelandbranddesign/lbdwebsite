Identity System

What brand
architecture means.

Brand Architecture is the complete system that defines how your business presents itself: your positioning in the market, the messaging that carries your value, the visual identity that makes you recognizable, and the voice and tone standards that keep everything consistent.

Without this infrastructure, every piece of marketing has to reinvent itself. With it, everything from a social post to a proposal reads like the same company.

LBD builds brand systems designed to outlast campaigns — clear enough for your team to maintain, flexible enough to grow with the business.

What's Included

Fixed scope.
Every deliverable
defined upfront.

  • Market positioning and differentiation statement
  • Messaging hierarchy (primary, secondary, supporting claims)
  • Brand voice and tone standards with examples
  • Visual identity: logo, color palette, typography, spacing
  • Brand usage guide and application rules
  • Tagline and key messaging copy
  • One-page brand summary for internal alignment
Who This Is For

Built for
businesses growing past founder-led everything.

Brand Architecture is for businesses that are adding team members, partners, or channels — and need their identity to hold together without the founder in the room.

It's also for businesses that have been in market for years and have accumulated an inconsistent brand across platforms, materials, and messages. This work cleans that up and establishes a single source of truth.

If your business could look like anyone's, this is the work that makes it yours.
