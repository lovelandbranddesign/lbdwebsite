The System

Seven movements.
One closed-loop
system.

Each movement is a productized engagement with fixed scope and a fixed fee. Together, they form a compound growth system — built in sequence, designed to work as a whole.

How the System Works

Sequence
matters.
Compound it.

The seven movements aren't a menu. They're a system — built in order, each one reinforcing the next. Most clients start with the MAP. What happens after that depends on what the MAP reveals.

Some clients move through all seven over 12–18 months. Some engage two or three and maintain from there. The system adapts. The sequence doesn't change.

Strategy always comes first. Advertising always comes last. Everything in between gets built in the order that creates the most durable foundation.
