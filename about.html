Our Story

We built the firm
we wished existed.

Seventeen years in brand and marketing strategy. Built from a moral filter, not a pitch deck. Based in Pikeville, Kentucky. Operating everywhere.

The Founder

Aaron
Loveland

Aaron studied vocal performance and songwriting at Berklee College of Music. Not because he planned to build a marketing firm, but because he wanted to understand how craft, structure, and discipline create things that last. Those instincts transferred completely.

He founded Loveland Brand Design in 2008. Formalized as an LLC in 2018. Built it on the belief that most businesses are underserved by the marketing industry. Oversold on tactics, undersupported on strategy, and left holding deliverables that don't connect.

LBD exists to fix that. Strategy first. Fixed scope. No hourly. No retainers. Work we can defend on its own merits.

"If we can't defend your work on its own merits, we're not the right fit. That's not a slogan. It's the decision filter."
Aaron Loveland, Founder
Production Partner

Josh
Trepiccione

Josh Trepiccione leads production at LBD. The infrastructure layer that turns strategy into executed deliverables at speed. He manages the technical stack, campaign deployment, and delivery workflows that allow LBD to operate with a two-person team while producing what most agencies require ten to do.

His role is the reason fixed scope is actually achievable. Strategy without production discipline is just a document. Josh is what makes it a system.

The Model

Why this
structure.

Most agencies sell time. The incentive is to use more of it. LBD sells outcomes. Fixed scope, fixed fee, defined deliverables. The incentive is to produce efficiently and well.

AI runs through every engagement at the production layer. It's how we compress weeks into days without compressing quality. It's infrastructure, not a feature.

The result: clients know exactly what they're getting before we start. No surprises. No scope creep. No "that's an add-on."

Where We Operate

Pikeville, Kentucky.
By choice.

LBD is headquartered in Pikeville, KY. Embedded in the Catalyze EKY entrepreneurial ecosystem. This region is underserved by the kind of marketing infrastructure most businesses elsewhere take for granted. We're building it here first.

We work with clients across the country. The EKY base isn't a limitation. It's a position. The businesses doing the hardest, most essential work are often the ones with the least marketing support. That's the problem we exist to solve.
