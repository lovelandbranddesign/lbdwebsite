How We Work

The Foundation

Strategy
before
execution.

Most agencies start with tactics and figure out strategy later. LBD inverts that. The MAP comes first — every time, for every client.

The MAP is a comprehensive strategic document: personas, voice of customer research, competitive intelligence, messaging hierarchy, funnel architecture, positioning, tech stack audit, and a 90-day campaign calendar. It's the blueprint every downstream decision runs on.

Nothing gets built on a foundation we haven't verified.

The Business Model

Fixed scope.
Fixed fee.
Every time.

Before a single deliverable is built, you know what you're getting, what it costs, and when it's done. The proposal is the contract.

No hourly billing. No retainers. No "that's an add-on." We produce defined deliverables — not time. The incentive is to work efficiently and well, not to maximize hours.

Digital Advertising and Prospecting are billed monthly because the work is ongoing — but they're priced as fixed-scope monthly engagements, not open-ended retainers.

  • Scope defined and agreed before work begins
  • Fee locked — no surprises mid-engagement
  • Deliverables specified, not time estimates
  • Timeline committed upfront
  • No hourly billing. Ever.
The AI Layer

AI as
production
infrastructure.

AI runs through every LBD engagement at the production layer. It's how a two-person firm delivers what most agencies require ten people to produce.

We use AI to accelerate research synthesis, content development, campaign architecture, data analysis, and reporting. Not to replace strategic judgment — to compress the time between decision and deliverable.

Clients don't pay for AI as a feature. It's in the model. It's why fixed-scope pricing is achievable at the quality level we maintain.

  • Research and competitive analysis
  • Content production at scale
  • Campaign architecture and testing
  • Reporting and performance synthesis
  • Workflow automation and CRM integration
What We Don't Do

We're
not for everyone.

LBD doesn't sell hours. Doesn't take clients whose work we can't defend. Doesn't build before the strategy is locked. Doesn't do quick-turnaround logo work or social media management without a strategic foundation underneath it.

If you need someone to execute tactics without a MAP, that's not us. If you need a firm that'll tell you what you want to hear, that's definitely not us.

If you want to build something that works — a system with real infrastructure, measurable performance, and a strategy behind every decision — that's exactly what we do.
