How We Choose

The moral
filter is real.

LBD maintains a small roster by design. Every client gets the full attention of the firm — not an account manager and a junior team. We turn down work that doesn't fit the standard, even when it's profitable.

The clients below are doing work that matters in their markets. Some are building trades businesses with generational stakes. Some are practitioners doing care-forward work. Some are operators in overlooked places building something the region needs. All of them are doing work we can stand behind.
